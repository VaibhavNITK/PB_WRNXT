Small Intro

• Welcome to Vatsalya Women and Child Clinic, where expertise meets compassion in the realm of women's health.
• Driven by a commitment to empower and prioritize the well-being of women, Dr. B Poornima R. Bhat is a distinguished Obstetrician and Gynecologist dedicated to delivering comprehensive and personalized healthcare. With an extensive background in OBG, she brings a wealth of knowledge and skill to every patient interaction. From routine gynecological examinations to guiding women through the miraculous journey of pregnancy and childbirth, she combines cutting-edge medical practices with a warm, patient-centered approach.
• At Vatsalya Women and Child Clinic, we understand the unique and evolving healthcare needs of women at every stage of life. Dr. Poornima R. Bhat fosters an environment of trust and open communication, ensuring that each patient feels heard, respected, and actively involved in their healthcare decisions.
• As a leader in the field, Dr. Poornima R. Bhat stays at the forefront of advancements in obstetrics and gynecology, embracing a commitment to ongoing education and innovation. Your health and comfort are our top priorities, and we are here to provide expert guidance, support, and care to enhance your overall well-being.
• Embark on a journey of health and vitality with Dr. Poornima R. Bhat, your partner in women's wellness. Your unique needs are our focus, and we look forward to accompanying you on the path to optimal health.
